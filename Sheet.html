How It Works

Learn how to play, earn, and win with SheeTY's blockchain-powered platform

Step 1: Play the Game

Spin for your ship and enter the SheeTY Abyss. Earn points by destroying meteors. A Free option for entry into our raffles!

Step 2: Collect Points

Obtain a minumum of 2000 points to enter the raffle. Must obtain enough points in one game session to be eligible for entry. IP addresses and Google accounts will be logged.

Step 3: Survive

Dodge obstacles and bullets to survive for higher scores. If you choose not to play, you can pay $10-$20 depending on which tier raffle you choose to join. Raffle terms will be listed.

Step 4: Claim Rewards

Rise through the ranks for the leaderboard for exclusive rewards! Redeem your scores and enter a raffle for big wins and REAL prizes.

Step 5: Win Real Prizes

Multi-Tier Raffles offered all FREE to enter! Random winners get PS5s, crypto, or exclusive SheeTY NFTs via Chainlink VRF.
