How It Works

Learn how to play, earn, and win with SheeTY

Step 1: Play the Game

Choose your ship and enter the SheeTY Abyss. Earn points by blasting meteors and dodging obstacles as you progress.

Step 2: Earn or Buy $SHEET

Earn $SHEET by reaching score thresholds or winning matches. You can also purchase $SHEET with crypto or fiat payment options.

Step 3: Enter Raffles

Use your $SHEET or points to enter raffles. Participate weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly for high-value rewards and chances to win.

Step 4: Win Real Prizes

Raffle winners are selected via Chainlink VRF. Claim prizes like PS5s, crypto, or NFTs if you're among the lucky few!
